# FallPreventionPlan.com - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

**Prepared for:** Geoff Angell, DPT, Active for Life Rehabilitation
**Prepared by:** Business Builders
**Date:** May 2026
**Target prototype build environment:** Replit (AI-assisted, custom stack)
**Target launch:** August / September 2026

---

## 1. Product Overview

### 1.1 What it is
A subscription-based membership platform that teaches senior adults how to reduce their risk of falling, what to do if a fall occurs, and which products and home modifications support fall prevention. The platform pairs structured educational content with a live community layer (group exercise classes, weekly Q&A, optional 1-on-1 training, and a content library).

### 1.2 Why it exists
Falls are the leading cause of injury and injury-related death in U.S. seniors (14M falls, 5M injuries, 34K deaths, $50B in healthcare costs annually). Current resources address fall prevention in fragments. The Fall Prevention Plan delivers a single, complete, easy-to-follow strategy backed by ongoing coaching and community.

### 1.3 Success criteria (12 months post-launch)
- 1,000+ one-time or subscription units sold
- 500+ active monthly subscribers
- 40-50 concierge members
- $10,000-$12,000 monthly recurring revenue
- Average subscriber retention of 10-14 months

## 2. Users and Roles

| Role | % of users | Job to be done | Access |
|---|---|---|---|
| **One-Time Member** | 15-25% | Learn the strategies to avoid falling, one purchase, no ongoing community access | Educational modules only |
| **Subscription Member (PRIMARY)** | 60-70% | Learn the strategies, plus ongoing access to live classes, weekly Q&A, 1-on-1 add-ons, and content library | Full platform |
| **Concierge Member** | 10-15% | Everything in Subscription, plus proactive outreach and personalized oversight from Geoff | Full platform + concierge dashboard |
| **Admin (Geoff / BB team)** | n/a | Create and manage content, host live sessions, manage members, run reports | Admin console |

**Primary user persona:** ~72-year-old senior adult, motivated to stay independent, moderate digital literacy, prefers large text and simple navigation.

## 3. MVP Scope (V1, launch-ready)

### 3.1 Public site (unauthenticated)
- Homepage with clear value proposition and "money moment" CTA: take the Fall Risk Self-Assessment or purchase access
- About / Geoff's story (credibility: 20 years PT, mobile practice owner)
- Free preview of Plan structure (titles of 10-Point and 5-Point plans, with locked content)
- Pricing page with three tiers (One-Time, Subscription, Concierge)
- Sign-up flow
- Login
- Contact

### 3.2 Member platform (authenticated)
**Educational content (all members):**
1. Introduction to Falls (written + supporting visuals)
2. Fall Risk Self-Assessment Tool (interactive, scored, generates personalized risk profile)
3. The 10-Point Plan to Avoid Falling (10 modules, written + video)
4. The 5-Point Plan If a Fall Occurs (5 modules, written + video)
5. Appendix A: Fall Prevention Equipment (with Amazon affiliate links)
6. Appendix B: Home Safety Modifications (with Amazon affiliate links)

**Community layer (Subscription + Concierge):**
- Live Group Exercise Classes (3-5x / week), accessible via in-app session link
- Weekly Live Q&A / Program Review (Wednesdays, 2 hrs)
- Library of recorded classes, past Q&A sessions, articles, and interviews
- 1-on-1 Training booking (paid add-on, $25 / 15-min unit confirmed in latest pricing)

**Concierge-only:**
- Concierge dashboard with notes, check-in history, and direct messaging with Geoff
- Scheduled outreach cadence configurable per member

### 3.3 Account and billing
- Email + password authentication, plus Sign in with Google as a secondary option (simplest path for the 72+ audience; SSO-only adds friction)
- Password reset via email
- Stripe-powered checkout
- Three SKUs: One-Time ($50), Subscription ($9-10 / month, to be finalized), Concierge (tiered, to be finalized)
- 1-on-1 sessions purchased as one-off line items
- Member account page: subscription status, billing history, update card, cancel
- Automatic dunning on failed payments

### 3.4 Admin console (Geoff and BB team)
- Member list with subscription status, tier, signup date, last login
- Manual upgrade / downgrade / refund
- Content management for modules, library items, and emails
- Schedule and publish upcoming live classes and Q&A sessions
- Concierge member list with outreach log
- Export to CSV

### 3.5 Communications
- Transactional email (signup confirm, password reset, payment receipts, class reminders)
- Marketing email broadcasts to all members or filtered by tier
- In-app notifications for upcoming live sessions

## 4. Out of Scope (V1)

The following are explicitly deferred to V2+ to keep the launch lean:

- Native mobile apps (iOS / Android). The web app will be mobile-responsive instead.
- Peer-to-peer member messaging or forums
- Gamification (badges, streaks, leaderboards)
- Multi-language support
- Affiliate / referral program
- Custom-built video player (we use embedded Vimeo or YouTube)
- AI-powered personalization beyond the static assessment scoring
- HIPAA-grade PHI handling. The platform will not capture clinical health intake data in V1.

## 5. Tech Stack and Integrations

### 5.1 Recommended stack (Replit-friendly)
| Layer | Choice | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Frontend | Next.js (React) | Standard, transferable, AI-assisted scaffolding works well |
| Backend / API | Next.js API routes or Node / Express | Co-located with frontend, simple |
| Database | PostgreSQL (via Neon or Supabase) | Standard relational, scales, free tier available |
| Auth | Clerk or Auth.js | Drop-in, supports email + Google, age-appropriate UX |
| Hosting | Vercel (frontend) + Neon / Supabase (DB) | Replit can deploy directly. InMotion is currently registered for the domain; DNS will be pointed to Vercel. |
| File storage | S3-compatible bucket (Cloudflare R2 or AWS S3) | For images and PDFs |

### 5.2 Third-party integrations
| Purpose | Tool | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Payments and subscriptions | **Stripe** | Required. ~2.9% + 30 cents per transaction. |
| Video hosting (on-demand) | **Vimeo Pro** ($20 / mo) | Cleaner than YouTube, no ads, controlled player |
| Live group sessions | **Zoom** ($15-25 / mo) | Most familiar to seniors, lowest learning curve. Riverside is an alternative if recording quality is a priority. |
| 1-on-1 booking | **Calendly** ($0-12 / mo) | Embed inside member dashboard |
| Email (transactional) | **Resend** or **Postmark** | Developer-friendly, reliable |
| Email (marketing) | **MailerLite** or **ConvertKit** | Geoff-friendly UI for sending broadcasts |
| CRM | **HubSpot Starter** | Tracks member lifecycle, syncs from Stripe |
| Affiliate links | **Amazon Associates** | Already understood. Links live in Appendix content. |
| Analytics | **Plausible** or **PostHog** | Privacy-friendly. Tracks page views, signups, conversion funnel, retention. |

## 6. UX Requirements (Senior-Friendly)

These are non-negotiable for V1.

- Minimum body font size 18px; headings 24-36px
- High contrast color palette (WCAG AA minimum, targeting AAA where practical)
- Large touch targets (44px minimum)
- Plain-English microcopy. No jargon.
- Primary action buttons clearly labeled with verbs ("Start Module 1", "Join Live Class")
- Avoid hidden navigation (no hamburger-only menus on desktop)
- Single column layouts for content reading
- Persistent "Help" link in the top nav
- Video player with large play, pause, and full-screen controls
- Inactivity-tolerant sessions (long timeout)
- "Print this page" option on key reference content (10-Point Plan, 5-Point Plan)

## 9. Compliance and Security

- **PCI:** Handled by Stripe. We never touch raw card data.
- **GDPR / CCPA:** Add cookie consent banner and privacy policy. Build a "Delete my account" pathway.
- **HIPAA:** Out of scope. The platform will not collect PHI. The Fall Risk Self-Assessment is positioned as educational, not diagnostic.
- **Accessibility:** WCAG 2.1 AA target.

## 10. Open Questions (To Resolve Before Build Lock)

1. **Pricing finalization.** Subscription monthly: $9 or $10? Concierge: how many sub-tiers and at what price points?
2. **One-Time Member access scope.** Do they get any community access (e.g. one free class) as an upsell hook, or strictly educational content?
3. **Free Fall Risk Self-Assessment as a lead magnet.** Make it available without signup to drive top-of-funnel?
4. **Annual subscription option.** Offer a discounted annual SKU alongside monthly?
5. **Free trial.** 7 or 14 days, or none?
6. **Refund / cancellation policy.** What's the policy text?
7. **Content drip vs all-at-once.** Release all 10-Point modules at once, or drip weekly?
8. **Video production timeline.** When are exercise / instructional videos ready? Can we launch with written content and Loom-style placeholder videos and swap later?
9. **Concierge service definition.** What exactly does Geoff commit to per week per concierge member? (Calls? Texts? Custom plans?)
10. **Trademark / IP.** Is "10-Point Plan to Avoid Falling" trademarked or pending? Affects how we label and protect it.
11. **Email platform preference.** Geoff's comfort level with HubSpot vs simpler tools like MailerLite?
12. **Domain.** Launch on FallPreventionPlan.com or TheFallPreventionPlan.com? Redirect the other.

## 11. Prototype Goals (What we'll review together)

The Replit prototype will demonstrate:
- The signup / login flow
- A locked vs unlocked module view (gating logic)
- The Fall Risk Self-Assessment end-to-end
- One fully built module from the 10-Point Plan (as a content template)
- A live session listing page
- Stripe checkout in test mode

Everything else will be wireframed for review but not fully wired. The goal is to validate the user journey and senior-friendly design choices before going deep on every module.
